Labelled form control for contact / intake forms.

```jsx
<Field label="Nome" placeholder="Seu nome completo" required />
<Field label="E-mail" type="email" placeholder="voce@empresa.com.br" />
<Field label="Como podemos ajudar" textarea rows={5} hint="Descreva brevemente sua demanda." />
```

Uppercase Jost label, ivory fill, crisp 4px corners, emerald focus ring. Pass `textarea` for multi-line. Designed for light surfaces.
